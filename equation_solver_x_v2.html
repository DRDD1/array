Equation solver in x (real roots)

Enter a full equality in one variable x, choose a search interval, and the page will look for multiple real solutions. It scans the interval for candidates and then refines each root numerically.

Use ^ for powers. You can also paste Unicode symbols like π, ×, ÷, − and the solver will normalise them. If you omit =, the solver assumes you entered f(x)=0.
Supported constants: pi, e (also accepts π). Supported functions: sin cos tan asin acos atan exp ln log sqrt abs floor ceil. Multiplication is best written explicitly (2*x), but common cases like 2x and (x+1)(x-1) are handled.
